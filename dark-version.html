Swedavias UtryckningsrapportSystem (SUS)

Swedavia's Emergency Report System (SUS) at Arlanda Airport is undergoing a redesign to enhance emergency response capabilities. The focus is on user-friendly interfaces, efficient reporting, and real-time updates. I was actively involved in the site's redesign to improve the user experience, collaborating closely with a cross-functional team and clients from the beginning. To explore more about the case, visit the case study page on my Dribbble site.

Nilo App

Nilo is an online meeting app focused on enhancing collaboration and decision-making through effective knowledge sharing. As a UX designer, I'm part of the team developing its mobile version. This ongoing project aims to improve the app's accessibility and user experience. Updates and detailed case studies will be shared soon to highlight our progress and the impact of our work.

Hugo Tech

HUGO is at the forefront of redefining last-mile delivery with its innovative use of autonomous robots, offering efficient, short-distance transportation for goods. Involved in their rebranding, I aimed to capture HUGO's blend of technological advancement and user-friendly design. Click to read more about this project on my Dribbble page.

Floria

For the Floria project, a night flower delivery store, I created the branding and identity in the early stage. This involved developing a unique visual language that captures the essence of Floria's service that delivering flowers under the moonlight. My focus was on crafting an identity that stands out in the competitive flower delivery market, emphasizing the beauty and serenity of nighttime deliveries. You can explore this case study on my Dribbble site.

Västtrafik To-Go App

This project was completed by a four-member group. We leveraged our UX research findings to modify the design of the “Västtrafik To-Go app” with the goal of resolving the issues identified during the user testing phase. I served as the scrum master and UX designer for this project. You can find more detailed case study about this project on my Dribbble page.

Museum Audio-tour App

This was my first UX design project, which involved creating a prototype for an audio-tour mobile application for museums. The primary goal of this app is to offer audio assistance to museum and art gallery visitors, regardless of age or gender. As the solo UX designer on this project, I managed the entire design process from initial concept to final delivery. Click to read more about this project on my Dribbble page.

Småtrollen Föräldrakooperativ

This project was conducted under the requirements of Småtrollen Föräldrakooperativ, a Swedish kindergarten. Four group members were involved; we conducted a heuristic evaluation of the current website and developed a design sample for the new version, specifically for mobile. I served as the main designer and photographer for the project. Explore this case study on my Dribbble site.

“Hej Molly”

The educational game app for kids, 'Hej Molly,' targets children aged 3-9 and focuses on helping them learn to read and write in Swedish and Chinese, as well as offering some mathematical practice. The project was recently initiated, and I am serving as the project leader and product designer. As of now, the project doesn't have a case study, but this will be updated once it's ready.
